Tea is the second most consumed beverage in the world after water, highlighting its universal appeal and cultural significance. Its popularity is rooted not only in its diverse flavors and traditions but also in its unique physiological effects. Tea contains caffeine, which provides a gentle energy boost, and L-theanine, an amino acid that promotes relaxation and focus. This combination creates a balanced effect, making tea a versatile drink for both energizing mornings and calming evenings, cherished by people across the globe.
